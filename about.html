Our Story

Born from necessity, built with purpose

üî• 2020

The Spark

During the catastrophic 2020 fire season, our founders experienced firsthand the frustration of scattered, delayed, and often contradictory wildfire information. Families were forced to make life-or-death evacuation decisions based on incomplete data.

üí° 2021

The Vision

We realized that the technology existed to provide real-time wildfire intelligence - it just wasn't accessible to the people who needed it most. We began building a platform that would change that.

üíª 2022

Building the Foundation

Working with fire professionals, emergency managers, and data scientists, we developed the algorithms and interfaces that would become Eye on the Fire. Hundreds of hours of testing with real communities.

üöÄ 2023

Launch & Growth

Eye on the Fire went live during the 2023 fire season. Within months, we were serving millions of users across wildfire-prone regions, providing critical information when they needed it most.

üìà 2024

Expanding Impact

Today, we serve over 7 million users across all 50 states. Our platform has become a trusted resource for families, emergency managers, and first responders nationwide.

‚≠ê 2025

The Future

We're continuously innovating with new features like AI-powered risk assessment, community-driven reporting, and enhanced mobile experiences. Our commitment to free, accessible wildfire intelligence remains unwavering.
