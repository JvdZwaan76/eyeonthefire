About Eye on the Fire

Learn more about our mission to keep you safe and informed.

Our Mission

At Eye on the Fire LLC, we are committed to empowering communities with real-time fire updates, expert prevention tips, and a platform for sharing fire safety resources through our community hub. Founded in 2025, our mission is to enhance fire safety awareness and preparedness across Montana and beyond. We strive to be a trusted partner in protecting lives and properties by delivering accurate, timely information and fostering a community dedicated to fire prevention. Learn more about fire safety from the National Fire Protection Association (NFPA).

Our Team

Eye on the Fire LLC was founded by a team of fire safety advocates with a passion for protecting communities. Our team includes experts in fire prevention, technology, and community engagement, working together to provide the most reliable and accessible fire safety resources. We are based in Helena, Montana, and are proud to serve our local and national communities. For more information on fire safety regulations, visit Cal Fire.

Official Recognition

Eye on the Fire LLC is a Montana-based company, officially recognized by the State of Montana. We are proud to share that:

  • Eye on the Fire LLC duly filed its Articles of Organization for Domestic Limited Liability Company on March 19, 2025.
  • The company was authorized to transact business in Montana with a term of perpetual duration.
  • As of March 25, 2025, the records of the Montana Secretary of State indicate that Eye on the Fire LLC is in good standing under the laws of the State of Montana.
  • This certification was issued by Christi Jacobsen, Montana Secretary of State, under Certificate Number: 68324532.

You can verify our status through the Montana Secretary of State’s Business Services. For inquiries regarding our tax status, please contact the Montana Department of Revenue at (406) 444-6900.

Our Commitment to Fire Safety
