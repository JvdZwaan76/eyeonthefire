About Eye on the Fire: Your Trusted Wildfire Tracker

Discover our mission to empower communities with real-time US wildfire tracking, safety resources, and preparedness guides. Stay informed, stay safe.

Explore the Live Wildfire Map

Our Mission: Protecting Communities Nationwide

At Eye on the Fire, we are dedicated to providing accurate, real-time wildfire tracking to help communities across the United States stay safe during the wildfire season. Our advanced US wildfire map consolidates data from trusted sources like NASA FIRMS and the National Interagency Fire Center (NIFC), delivering actionable insights to residents, firefighters, and emergency managers. By offering comprehensive wildfire safety and preparedness resources, we aim to empower every American to protect their homes and loved ones.

Why Choose Eye on the Fire?

With wildfire risks on the rise, having a reliable wildfire tracker is essential. Here’s why Eye on the Fire stands out:

  • Real-Time Updates: Our interactive fire map provides up-to-the-minute data on active wildfires nationwide.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Easily locate fires near you with intuitive map controls and geolocation features.
  • Comprehensive Guides: Access expert wildfire preparedness resources, from home hardening to evacuation planning.
  • Nationwide Coverage: Monitor wildfires in every state, from California to the East Coast.

Our Expertise: Driven by Data and Commitment

Eye on the Fire is powered by a team passionate about wildfire safety and technology. We aggregate data from leading sources like NASA FIRMS and NIFC, ensuring our real-time fire monitoring is both accurate and reliable. Our commitment to excellence drives us to deliver user-friendly tools and resources that make a difference in communities nationwide.

Join Us in Staying Safe

Whether you’re tracking fires near you, preparing your home with our wildfire safety guides, or seeking to collaborate, Eye on the Fire is here to help. Explore our platform or reach out to learn more.
