About Eye on the Fire

Your trusted resource for real-time US wildfire tracking and fire safety. Learn how we empower communities with interactive maps and preparedness guides.

Explore Live Wildfire Map

Our Mission

At Eye on the Fire, our mission is to provide accurate, real-time wildfire tracking across the United States, empowering individuals and communities to stay informed and safe. By consolidating data from trusted sources like NASA FIRMS and NIFC, we deliver an interactive US wildfire map that helps you monitor fires near you and access critical safety resources.

We are committed to enhancing wildfire preparedness through comprehensive guides on home hardening, defensible space, and emergency planning, ensuring you have the tools to protect what matters most.

Why Choose Eye on the Fire?

  • Real-Time Wildfire Monitoring: Our interactive map provides up-to-date wildfire data, helping you track fires near you anywhere in the US.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Easily navigate our map with controls to locate your position, zoom into regions, and explore fire details.
  • Comprehensive Safety Resources: Access expert guides on wildfire prevention, home safety, and emergency preparedness to protect your family and property.
  • Trusted Data Sources: We partner with NASA FIRMS, NIFC, and other official channels to ensure reliable information.

Join thousands of users who rely on Eye on the Fire for situational awareness during the US fire season.

Our Community

Eye on the Fire is more than a platform—it's a community dedicated to wildfire safety and resilience. We collaborate with firefighters, emergency managers, and volunteers across the US to provide resources that make a difference.

Whether you're a homeowner, a community leader, or a concerned citizen, we invite you to join us in promoting fire-smart practices and supporting wildfire-affected areas. Explore our resources to get involved.
