Our Mission

At Eye on the Fire LLC, we are dedicated to providing real-time fire updates, prevention tips, and community-shared fire safety resources. Our mission is to empower individuals and communities to stay ahead of the flames, ensuring safety and preparedness in the face of fire risks.

Official Recognition

Eye on the Fire LLC is a Montana-based company, officially recognized by the State of Montana. We are proud to share that:

  • Eye on the Fire LLC duly filed its Articles of Organization for Domestic Limited Liability Company on March 19, 2025.
  • The company was authorized to transact business in Montana with a term of perpetual duration.
  • As of March 25, 2025, the records of the Montana Secretary of State indicate that Eye on the Fire LLC is in good standing under the laws of the State of Montana.
  • This certification was issued by Christi Jacobsen, Montana Secretary of State, under Certificate Number: 68324532.

For inquiries regarding our tax status, please contact the Montana Department of Revenue at (406) 444-6900.
