The LA and Pacific Palisades Fires of January 2025

The Palisades and Eaton Fires, which started on January 7, 2025, caused unprecedented devastation in the Los Angeles area, including Pacific Palisades, Malibu, Altadena, and Pasadena. These fires destroyed over 12,000 structures and claimed at least 24 lives, according to Los Angeles County officials. At Eye on the Fire LLC, we are committed to raising awareness about the importance of fire safety and preparedness. Below are personal photos and videos capturing the impact of these fires, shared to highlight the need for vigilance and community support.
