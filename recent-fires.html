The LA and Pacific Palisades Fires of January 2025

The Palisades and Eaton Fires, which ignited on January 7, 2025, caused unprecedented devastation across the Los Angeles area, including Pacific Palisades, Malibu, Altadena, and Pasadena. Fueled by dry conditions and strong winds, these fires destroyed over 12,000 structures, claimed at least 24 lives, and displaced thousands, according to Los Angeles County officials. Fire Safety Tips for Urban Wildfires

The LA and Pacific Palisades fires highlight the unique challenges of urban wildfires. Here are some fire safety tips to help you prepare:

  • Create a Defensible Space: Clear flammable materials within 30 feet of your home. Learn more from Cal Fire.
  • Prepare an Evacuation Plan: Plan multiple escape routes. Visit Ready.gov for tips.
  • Stay Informed: Monitor live updates above or on our homepage.
  • Emergency Kit: Include water, food, masks, and documents. See Red Cross.

For official updates, check LA County Emergency Services.
