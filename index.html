Prepare. Plan. Protect.

Wildfire preparedness can be the difference between losing everything and protecting what matters most. Our comprehensive safety guides are developed by fire professionals and emergency management experts.

  • Create Defensible Space

    Clear vegetation within 30-100 feet of your home using the zone system recommended by fire professionals.

  • Harden Your Home

    Use fire-resistant materials for roofing, siding, and decking. Install ember-resistant vents and screens.

  • Plan Multiple Escape Routes

    Know at least two ways to evacuate your neighborhood and practice your evacuation plan regularly.

  • Prepare Emergency Kit

    Keep important documents, medications, and supplies ready for rapid evacuation at a moment's notice.
