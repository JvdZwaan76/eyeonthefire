Wildfire Prevention & Home Safety

Protecting your property starts long before a wildfire approaches. Implementing fire prevention strategies like home hardening and creating defensible space are the most effective ways to increase your home's chance of survival. These resources help you build your wildfire action plan.

Defensible Space Zones

Learn how to create and maintain zones 0, 1, and 2 around your home to reduce flammable materials and stop fire spread. Essential for California homeowners.

Home Hardening Techniques

Discover methods to make your home more resistant to embers and flames, including non-combustible roofing, siding, screened vents, and dual-pane windows.

Fire-Smart Landscaping

Choose appropriate vegetation and spacing for plants near your home. Use fire-resistant native plants and maintain irrigation to enhance your defensible space.
